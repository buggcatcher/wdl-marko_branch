Drink and Paint Florence and Countryside

Experience

Flex your creative muscles as you gaze upon a beautiful vista of Florence’s rolling hills from Marignolle, just outside the city center. At the garden of local microbrewery La Birraia di Marignolle you will learn how to paint the idyllic scene before you from a professional artist as you sample beer or wine and bruschetta. Spend an evening enjoying this beautiful natural environment in a unique way.

Payment on site. Best price guaranteed.

*On friday we do the experience whit mixed groups only and the cost is €50 for each adults*

Starts at 5pm or 6pm (depending on the weather)
