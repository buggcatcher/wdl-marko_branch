Il Kebab dal Cuore Toscano

Icchebab Il Kebab dal Cuore Toscano is a standout culinary destination in Florence, offering a unique fusion of traditional Turkish kebab with authentic Tuscan flavors. Situated in Piazza Dalmazia, this eatery prides itself on using locally sourced, high-quality ingredients to craft its dishes, ensuring a fresh and flavorful experience for every customer.

The menu features a variety of options, including freshly baked paninis and piadinas filled with tender kebab meat, crisp vegetables, and house-made sauces. For those seeking a heartier meal, the kebab plates come generously portioned with meat, fresh veggies, and signature sauces. Vegetarian choices are also available , catering to diverse dietary preferences.

Icchebab's commitment to quality and authenticity has earned it high praise from patrons, boasting a 4.9 out of 5 rating on platforms like Restaurant Guru and Tripadvisor. Customers frequently highlight the exceptional taste and the dedication of the staff in delivering a memorable dining experience.

Whether you're a local or a visitor exploring Florence, Icchebab offers a delightful culinary journey that combines the rich traditions of Tuscan and Turkish cuisines.
