Cibreo Places

Immerse yourself in the culinary universe of Cibrèo in Florence, where tradition harmoniously blends with innovation across a collection of distinctive venues:

Cibrèo Ristorante – An elegant dining experience offering refined Tuscan cuisine with a contemporary twist. Average price: €65–€110 per person.

Cibrèo Trattoria (Il Cibrèino) – A cosy setting serving classic Florentine dishes in a relaxed atmosphere. Average price: €35–€45 per person.

Ciblèo – A unique Tuscan-Oriental eatery blending local ingredients with Japanese, Korean, and Chinese influences. Average price: €35–€50 per person.

Cibrèo Caffè (Sant'Ambrogio) – A charming café offering a selection of traditional dishes and signature cocktails in a relaxed setting. Average price: €30–€50 per person.

Cibrèo Caffè at Helvetia & Bristol – A stylish café and cocktail bar offering light bites and drinks in an upscale environment. Average price: €31–€50 per person.

Cibrèo Ristorante & Cocktail Bar – A sophisticated venue combining contemporary Italian cuisine with expertly crafted cocktails. Average price: €65–€110 per person.

Teatro del Sale – A cultural hub combining live performances with a buffet-style dining experience. Average price: €30–€50 per person.

Each venue embodies the spirit of Cibrèo: exceptional quality, warm hospitality, and a celebration of Florentine culinary heritage.
